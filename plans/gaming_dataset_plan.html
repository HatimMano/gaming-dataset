🔬 Enhanced Quality Scoring Analysis

Gaming Density Optimization

Our multi-tier vocabulary scoring provides more nuanced quality assessment:

4x Weight for complex phrases
3x Weight for gameplay mechanics
2x Weight for technical/esports terms
50+ High-value phrase patterns
Example: "frame data analysis" (4x weight) vs "game" (1x weight). This ensures articles discussing complex mechanics score significantly higher than basic game descriptions.

Content Depth Metrics

New content depth scoring captures educational value beyond simple term frequency:

  • Section Analysis: Gameplay, Development, Reception sections get bonus scoring
  • Depth Indicators: Examples, comparisons, and explanations boost scores
  • Paragraph Quality: Optimal 50-200 word paragraphs indicate structured content
  • Explanation Ratio: "Because", "however", "therefore" indicate analytical depth

Phase-Specific Adaptations

Why Adaptive Scoring?
Current Issue: Using identical weights across all phases leads to suboptimal selection. A 2015 game design article with deep theoretical content might score poorly on "freshness" despite being perfectly relevant for Phase 1B.
Solution: Weight adjustment per phase ensures we capture the best content for each collection goal rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach.
